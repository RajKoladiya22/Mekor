how we work

From planning to installation with Mekor Pipe

We follow a structured process from technical consultation and design support, through material selection and fabrication, to final delivery and on-site installation. Every step ensures compliance, reliability, and long service life.

01. technical consultation

We begin with an in-depth discussion to understand project scope, flow requirements, pressure ratings, and compliance needs.

02. design & material planning

Our team selects pipe grades, diameters, and coatings. We provide layout drawings and fabrication plans to match project specifications.

03. production & execution

Precision manufacturing with strict quality checks, followed by packaging and delivery. On-site coordination ensures smooth installation.

04. inspection & handover

Final testing and verification ensure all standards are met. We hand over with documentation, warranties, and technical support.
