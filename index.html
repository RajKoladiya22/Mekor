Why Choose Mekor

Global projects choose Mekor for certified systems, full-spectrum supply, and export-ready coordination.

Infrastructure teams worldwide choose Mekor for its certified, system-based supply approach that goes beyond standalone products. We deliver complete, application-specific systems—pipes, fittings, valves, and accessories—backed by international certifications like ISO, WRAS, BIS, and ASTM, with full traceability. Our export-ready coordination ensures seamless documentation, customs clearance, and compliance with project specs. With global delivery tailored to local regulations, Mekor is the trusted partner for infrastructure projects from Europe to Africa and the Middle East.

About the Mekor

Global Systems Supplier for Infrastructure Projects

Mekor is a certified infrastructure material supplier serving global contractors, sourcing teams, and institutional buyers. We don’t trade products—we deliver complete, project-ready systems built for reliability, compliance, and scale. Our portfolio spans thermoplastic and metallic piping, valves, fittings, HVAC and fire systems, geosynthetics, and more. Every batch is export-compliant and traceable. ​ We operate across Western Europe, North America, MENA, Africa, Latin America, Southeast Asia, and island nations—supplying what builds the world.
